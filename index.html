Selena Gomez
selena@selenagomez.com
A global artist who continues to impact her audience with frank and authentic conversations.
Experience
Recording Artist
Interscope Records
An award-winning and celebrated pop artist with worldwide chart-topping albums like 'Rare' and 'Revival' among others. Gomez has six albums and seventeen singles. As a solo artist, Gomez has accumulated over 20 billion global streams.
Actress
Hollywood
Gomez starred in a lot of critically acclaimed movies like 'Monte Carlo', 'The Fundamentals of Caring' and 'Spring Breakers' among others.
Producer
July Moon Productions, Inc.
Gomez is the executive producer of the hit Netflix original series '13 Reasons Why' and the critically acclaimed Netflix docu-series “Living Undocumented” among other shows and movies.
Founder
Rare Beauty
rarebeauty.com
Gomez launched Rare beauty, a beauty company with a focus on mental health, one's uniqueness and inclusivity, in September 2020.
Philanthropist
Giving back has always been an important element in Gomez's life. She joined Michelle Obama’s 'When We All Vote' for the 2020 US presedential elections. She works very closely with the 'We Movement'. She was named the youngest goodwill Ambassador for UNICEF at 18 years old and continues to work with them. Gomez also works very closely with 'A21', an non-profit organization seeking to eliminate human trafficking.
Discography
Rare
Earlier in 2021, Gomez released her critically acclaimed album RARE which debuted at No. 1 on the 'Billboard 200', her third consecutive studio album to debut atop the chart. The album has been met with widespread critical acclaim.
Revival
Gomez's second studio album was met with widespread critical acclaim.
Singles
Gomez has had seventeen mega-successful singles. Some of them are 'It Ain’t Me' with Kygo, 'Bad Liar', 'Fetish', 'Wolves' with Marshmello, 'I Can’t Get Enough' with Benny Blanco, J Balvin and Tainy and 'Back to You'
